About Vault Easy

An international brokerage built around one account, not a maze of them.

Vault Easy is a Panama-incorporated forex and CFD brokerage founded in 2015. The idea hasn't changed since: give clients a single account for spot forex, CFD futures and CFD shares, priced transparently.

Our Story

A single window for financial and brokerage services.

Vault Easy was founded to give traders one place to manage execution and custody across spot forex, CFD futures and other globally traded instruments — instead of splitting activity across separate brokers and platforms for each market.

That's still the mandate today: an all-in-one account, commission-free and swap-free forex, and leverage tailored to how each client actually manages risk — offered with transparent, client-oriented service.

Principles

The things we won't compromise on.

Not a mission statement — a working checklist every new product is measured against before it ships.

Client capital comes first

Every product decision is weighed against a single question: does this serve the person whose capital is on the line, or the firm's balance sheet? When those interests conflict, the client wins.

Radical transparency

Commission and swap policy disclosed upfront — nothing about our pricing is designed to be hard to find.

One account, not several

Spot forex, CFD futures and CFD shares stay under a single account rather than forcing clients to split activity across platforms.

Leverage that fits the client

Up to 200:1, set according to each client's own risk management approach rather than one ratio for everyone.

Long-term partnership

Built for clients who plan to keep trading with us, not for a single deposit.
