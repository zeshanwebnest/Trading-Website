Copy Trading FAQ

Before you allocate.

Can I lose money copy trading?

Yes. Copy trading carries the same market risk as any other trading activity — past performance of a strategy provider does not guarantee future results.

What's the minimum allocation?

$500 per strategy, subject to your account's overall minimum deposit.

Can I stop following a strategy at any time?

Yes — allocations can be reduced or closed instantly with no lock-in period or exit fee.
