FAQ

Questions, answered directly.

What's the minimum deposit to open an account?

Our Essential tier opens from $200. Professional and Prime tiers carry higher minimums in exchange for tighter spreads and dedicated service — see the comparison table above for full detail.

Where is Vault Easy incorporated?

Vault Easy is incorporated in Panama. Our full account terms are available before you fund an account — ask our team for a copy if you haven't seen them.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals to the original funding method are processed within one business day. Bank wires may take an additional 1–3 days depending on your receiving institution.

Can I try a demo before funding a live account?

Every prospective client receives a fully-featured demo account with live pricing and no time limit, so you can test execution before committing capital.

What leverage is available?

Leverage of up to 200:1 is available, set to match your own risk management approach rather than a single fixed ratio for every client.
