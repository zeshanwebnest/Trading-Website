Accounts FAQ

Common questions about tiers.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect once your deposited balance meets the new tier's minimum — no new application required.

Is leverage the same across all instruments?

No. Maximum leverage varies by asset class and jurisdiction; forex majors carry the highest limits, indices and shares are typically lower.

Do Essential accounts pay commission?

No — Essential accounts trade on spread only. Professional and Prime accounts trade raw spread plus a disclosed per-lot commission, which is typically cheaper at higher volumes.

Not sure which tier fits your trading style?

Speak with our desk before you fund an account — no obligation.
