How Swap Works

Two things worth knowing about overnight positions.

Daily rollover

Positions held open past 22:00 UTC are subject to a swap charge or credit, based on the interest rate differential between the two currencies in the pair (or the underlying financing rate for CFDs).

Weekend triple swap

To account for the weekend, swap is applied at three times the daily rate on Wednesdays for most forex pairs — check your platform's contract specification for the exact instrument.
