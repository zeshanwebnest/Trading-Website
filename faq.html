FAQ

Questions, answered directly.

Grouped by topic. If you can't find what you need, our desk answers direct messages within one business day.

Accounts

Opening and managing an account.

What's the minimum deposit to open an account?

Our Essential tier opens from $200. Professional and Prime tiers carry higher minimums in exchange for tighter spreads and dedicated service.

Can I hold more than one account?

Yes. Clients may hold multiple accounts across tiers or base currencies under a single verified identity.

How long does verification take?

Most identity and address documents are reviewed within a few hours during business days, and always within one business day.

Trading

Execution, leverage and market access.

Do you operate a dealing desk?

No. Orders are routed directly to liquidity providers via our execution bridges — Vault Easy does not take the opposite side of client trades.

What leverage is available?

Leverage of up to 200:1 is available and is set to match your own risk management approach rather than a single fixed ratio for every client.

Why did my spread widen during news?

Spreads reflect live liquidity conditions. Around high-impact news releases, underlying market liquidity thins and spreads widen accordingly across the industry, not just at Vault Easy.

Payments

Funding and withdrawing.

What deposit methods do you accept?

Bank transfer, major debit and credit cards, and a range of supported digital wallets. Available methods vary slightly by region.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals to the original funding method are processed within one business day. Bank wires may take an additional 1–3 days depending on your receiving institution.

Are there deposit or withdrawal fees?

Vault Easy does not charge deposit or withdrawal fees. Your bank or payment provider may apply its own charges, which we don't control.

Security

How your account is handled.

Where is Vault Easy incorporated?

Vault Easy is incorporated in Panama. Full terms covering how accounts and funds are handled are provided before you open an account — ask our team for a copy if you haven't seen them.

Is leverage trading risky?

Yes. Trading forex, CFD futures and CFD shares on leverage carries a high level of risk and can result in losses exceeding your deposit. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose.

How is my account secured online?

Account access is protected by standard login authentication. Never share your login details, and enable any additional verification options available on your account.

Still have a question?

Our desk typically responds within one business day.
